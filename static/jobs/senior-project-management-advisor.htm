Senior Project Management Advisor

Advisicon, a professional services consulting company based in Vancouver, Washington seeks candidates for a Senior Project Management Advisor position.

NOTE: Automotive industry experience required.

Summary

A Senior PM Advisor will provide the customer with a blend of skills around EPM, technology, and strategic business analysis. The Senior PM Advisor will engage in managing large scale EPM deployments.

The Senior PM Advisor will identify customer business problems, map customer business problems to a solution, manage stakeholder expectations, manage on-site and remote deployment team activities, track deployment progress, conduct on-going risk analysis and issue management, assist in knowledge transfer, and facilitate the deployment of the services and technology described in the proposed solution.

  • Experience in the automotive industry is required to be considered for this position.
  • Experience with Microsoft EPM (2007 or 2010) and PMP credential is highly desired.
  • Preference will be given to candidates located in the Detroit, MI area, or to candidates who are willing to relocate or travel frequently to site.

Advisicon delivers strategic project control solutions using advanced project and portfolio management methodologies, custom application development and consultative training.
