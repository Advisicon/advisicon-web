Project Advisor, EPM Consultant, & Trainer

Advisicon, an international professional services consulting company based in Vancouver, Washington seeks candidates for a Project Advisor and Trainer in EPM Solutions position.

We are a 20 year company with a long standing history of supporting Project, Program and Portfolio management and helping companies leverage technologies to optimize their business.

The Project Advisor will provide the customer with a blend of skills around enterprise project management, technology, and strategic business analysis. This position will engage in business and technology discussions related to business value and end-to-end Microsoft technology solutions. They will identify customer business problems, map them to a solution, and facilitate the deployment of the services and technology described in the recommended solution. This position must also be able to train individuals on how to use the technology in a corporate and public environment.

Salary Range: DOE. Pay increases based on results and meeting goals and targets.

Summary

The Project Advisor position entails the following tasks, activities, and responsibilities:

  • Lead requirements gathering sessions guiding clients in identifying pain points and business needs
  • Map solution to client needs
  • Configure, customize and integrate EPM solution to meet client requirements
  • Document implementations, including business and technical requirements, configuration and design diagrams, and next steps
  • Lead consulting team to meet milestones
  • Manage engagement scope
  • Manage stakeholder expectations
  • Proactively communicate project progress to stakeholders
  • Identify continuous improvement areas
  • Identify/Manage risks/issues
  • Ability to lead teams in developing new policy or policy changes
  • Identify opportunities for cross sales during engagement
  • Conduct role-based training on Microsoft Project/Project Server and SharePoint, including Team Members, Project Managers, and Administrators
  • Manage multiple deadlines and objectives
  • Attend discovery calls for pre-sales for Advisicon and Advisicon partners
  • Potential travel, up to 25%
