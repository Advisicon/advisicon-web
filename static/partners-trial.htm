Project Management Institute

Advisicon is a Global REP of the PMI. Our clients have confidence knowing that they have chosen an organization that is qualified to provide the highest level of project management training, and the convenience of receiving PDUs. Advisicon has been recognized by PMI as a Global Registered Education Provider. Advisicon considers PMI one of our most important partners in advancing and supporting project managers and the project management profession. Advisicon regularly supports PMI chapters by sponsoring events, delivering educational and chapter keynote presentations at local, national and global PMI events.

A Global REP is an organization approved by PMI to issue PDUs for its training courses. PMI requires that PMPs must earn 60 PDUs every three years in order to maintain certification.

All participants in Advisicon’s instructor-led training can earn credit toward their PMI certifications or PDUs. Each hour of scheduled classroom participation qualifies for one PDU.
