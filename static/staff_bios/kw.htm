Kevin Williamson, MCTS, PMP
Senior Portfolio Advisor
Kevin is a Senior Portfolio Advisor with Advisicon, Inc. He has earned the sought after Project Management Professional (PMP) certification of the Project Management Institute (PMI). He is also a Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist (MCTS) in Microsoft Office Project and Project Server technologies.

Kevin’s diverse experience in developing and managing projects, programs, sales and operations in the business and non-profit sectors brings both depth and breadth to clients. He brings international project and program management experience from his work with humanitarian aid and development programs in the former Soviet Union and as the Director of Programs for Medical Teams International (formerly Northwest Medical Teams). Kevin has also worked with Xerox, and in financial services and distribution industries.
Kevin's broad background and passion for teaching enables him to relate project and portfolio management methodologies and best practices to a wide range of audiences and organizations.
