Nicole Brown
Social Media and Marketing Administrator

Nicole is the Marketing Advisor at Advisicon and brings a unique mix of creativity and organization to the Advisicon team. She is an accomplished marketing professional with extensive event planning and social media content creation experience. Nicole is highly driven and focused on achieving continuous, improved business performance in a team environment.

Nicole thrives on communicating key marketing messages in the most unique, effective manner possible. Having spent several years in the publishing industry as both a Marketing Specialist and a Director of Publications for a local, NW book publisher, Nicole perfected her copy writing and online ad campaign development and tracking skills. Nicole is mindful of the latest Project Management news, taking great interest in industry trending and competitive market analysis.

Nicole is passionate about bettering the communities where the Advisicon team lives and works. As an avid volunteer Nicole has donated her time to numerous Pacific NW organizations. She is currently an active volunteer with the Big Brothers, Big Sisters program, the Portland, Oregon Woodstock Neighborhood Association, and PMI Portland. Nicole also spent many years as a Program Director, Volunteer Coordinator and Marketing Assistant for a local, 501(c)(3) non-profit organization serving as the connection between volunteers and at-risk youth in the community.

Education

Nicole earned a double major in Marketing and Advertising Management from the Portland State University School of Business
